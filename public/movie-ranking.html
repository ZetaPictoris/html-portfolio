My Top Four Movies

According to Martin

1. Die Hard

The first of the Die Hard movie is arguably the best! Bruce Willis in his prime and the action packed scenes were great!

2. The Fifth Element

Another Bruce Willis film, this is classed as a sci-fi opera. Action, music, and a great cast.

3. Air Force One

This movie, starring Harrison Ford, is set on the US Presidents Airplane. It also stars Gary Oldman who portrays a rebel russian militia soldier.

4. The Matrix

Are we living in a simulation? This is what The Matrix suggests. Keanu Reeves expertly potrays NEO, Humanities Saviour.
